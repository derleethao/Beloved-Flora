About Beloved Flora

Beloved Flora was created by a husband and wife duo, Joshua & Der. This creative career was inspired by their late grandmother who was already a long-time florist. They are self-taught in the art of flower arrangements.

Their dream is to pursue a lifestyle that connects all the things they love to do. Their work is inspired by art, love and life. The duo specializes in weddings and events, with impactful designs that utilizes a thoughtfully, curated approach to flowers tailored to each of their clients and projects.
